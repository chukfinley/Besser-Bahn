<p><i>Better Bahn</i> is an app for finding cheaper split ticket options for Deutsche Bahn connections.</p><p><br><b>Functions:</b></p><ul><li>Analysis of DB links (short vbid links and long URLs)</li><li>Find the cheapest split ticket combination</li><li>Support for BahnCard discounts (25/50, 1st/2nd class)</li><li>Integration for Deutschland-Ticket</li><li>Direct booking links for tickets found (the top offer is the correct one)</li><li>Detailed price comparisons and savings calculations</li><li><b>No server costs: The app runs entirely locally on your device.</b></li></ul>
